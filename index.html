About Me

I am a Research Associate in Civil and Environmental Engineering at the University of Wisconsin–Madison. My research focuses on trustworthy artificial intelligence for transportation, connected and automated vehicles, and transportation digital twins.

I develop physics-enhanced learning and model-based control methods that improve the safety, interpretability, and real-world readiness of automated transportation systems.
